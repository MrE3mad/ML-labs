This dataset (StudentPerformanceFactors.csv) contains information about students and various academic, personal, and socio-economic factors that may influence their academic performance.
Each row represents an individual student, and each column represents a specific attribute related to study habits, background, or learning environment.
And the columns as following:

Academic Factors

Hours_Studied: Number of hours a student spends studying.

Attendance: Attendance rate of the student.

Previous_Scores: Scores obtained in previous examinations.

Exam_Score: Final exam score of the student.



Personal & Lifestyle Factors

Gender: Gender of the student.

Sleep_Hours: Average number of hours the student sleeps.

Motivation_Level: Self-reported motivation level.

Physical_Activity: Level of physical activity.

Learning_Disabilities: Indicates whether the student has learning disabilities.



Family & Socio-Economic Factors

Parental_Involvement: Level of parental involvement in education.

Family_Income: Income category of the studentâ€™s family.

Parental_Education_Level: Highest education level of the parents.

Distance_from_Home: Distance between home and school.



School & Environmental Factors

Access_to_Resources: Availability of learning resources.

Tutoring_Sessions: Number of tutoring sessions attended.

Teacher_Quality: Quality level of teachers.

School_Type: Type of school attended (public or private).

Extracurricular_Activities: Participation in extracurricular activities.

Peer_Influence: Influence of peers on academic behavior.

Internet_Access: Availability of internet access at home or school.
