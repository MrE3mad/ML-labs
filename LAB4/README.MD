The BMW Global sales (2018-2025) dataset,This dataset contains monthly sales data for BMW cars from 2018 to 2025. 
It shows how many cars were sold, their average prices, and the total revenue in different regions around the world.
Each row in the dataset represents the sales of one BMW model in a specific region during a specific month and year.

Column Description
1- YEAR: The year when the sales were recorded (2018–2025).
2- MONTH: The month of the sales (1–12).
3- REGION: The region where the cars were sold
4- MODEL: The BMW car model
5- UNITS-SOLD: Number of cars sold in that month and region.
6- Avg_Price_EUR: Average price of the car in Euros.
7- Revenue_EUR: Total revenue generated (Units_Sold × Avg_Price_EUR).
8- BEV_Share: Share of electric vehicles in the market
9- Premium_Share: Percentage of the premium car market share.
10- GDP_Growth: Economic growth rate (%) of the region.
11- Fuel_Price_Index: Index that represents fuel price level.
